Official Minecraft Server Rules

By joining ProsperiaMC, you agree to these rules and staff decisions.

1) CHEATING & EXPLOITS

Not allowed:

  • hacked clients
  • x-ray / ESP
  • macros / autoclickers
  • dupes
  • anti-AFK bypasses
  • any unintended mechanic

Litematica Printer and easyPlace are allowed. However, if configured too aggressively, they may be limited by the anticheat and cause lagbacks or failed placements.

If it bypasses intended gameplay, it is not allowed.

2) CLAIMS & WILDERNESS

In wilderness and in claims where you have permissions — everything is allowed.

If any part of a build, farm, storage, villager setup, redstone, or wall is outside the claim, that part is fully lootable and destroyable, even if it only extends by 1 block.

3) COMBAT & RAIDING

Allowed:

  • PvP in wilderness
  • raiding in wilderness
  • looting in wilderness
  • TP-killing
  • ambushes

Not allowed:

  • TP-trapping
  • combat logging
  • safe-zone abuse
  • claim-edge immunity abuse
  • repeated PvP harassment

TP-killing is allowed. Using teleport itself as an unavoidable death trap is not.

4) CHAT & PLAYER CONDUCT

Not allowed:

  • spam
  • harassment
  • threats
  • NSFW content
  • impersonation
  • advertising
  • harmful statements involving self-harm, suicide, or real-life violence
  • messages intended to distress

Serious safety-related chat violations may lead to instant mute or ban.

5) ECONOMY & FARMS

Not allowed:

  • using alt accounts
  • AFK profit abuse
  • market manipulation
  • bypassing taxes, limits, or cooldowns
  • unintended infinite profit loops
  • passive systems made only for unfair profit

Unfair gains may be removed, wiped, or rolled back.

6) STAFF & INVESTIGATIONS

Not allowed:

  • refusing freeze checks
  • hiding evidence
  • moving illegal items
  • using alts during punishment
  • interfering with moderation

Players must cooperate with staff investigations.

7) LOOPHOLES

Using wording loopholes, plugin behavior, or technicalities to justify obvious abuse is not allowed.

Staff enforce by intent and impact, not by literal wording alone.

Official Server Punishment Policy

All punishments on ProsperiaMC follow an escalation ladder based on severity, prior history, intent, scale, and server impact.

Punishments may include warnings, mutes, temporary bans, long-term bans, permanent bans, IP bans, balance resets, inventory wipes, storage wipes, transfer reversals, and rollback of farms & builds.

1) CHEATING & EXPLOITS

Includes hacked clients, x-ray, ESP, macros, autoclickers, anti-AFK bypasses, duping, and exploit abuse.

Typical punishment ladder:

  • 1st offense → 7–14 day ban
  • 2nd offense → 30–60 day ban
  • 3rd offense → permanent ban or IP ban

Additional enforcement: Any gains from cheating may be fully removed, including: x-ray mined ores, duped items, exploit-generated items, unfair balances, linked alt storage and wealth. This may include full balance resets, inventory wipes, and transfer rollbacks.

2) CLAIMS, THEFT & GRIEFING

Unauthorized access, stealing, claim griefing, traps inside claims, and indirect damage.

Typical punishment ladder:

  • 1st offense → 3–5 day ban
  • 2nd offense → 10–14 day ban
  • 3rd offense → 30+ day ban

Severe griefing, mass theft, or repeated targeting may escalate directly.

3) COMBAT ABUSE

Includes TP-trapping, combat logging, safe-zone abuse, claim-edge immunity abuse, and repeated PvP harassment.

Typical punishment ladder:

  • 1st offense → warning or 12h–1 day ban
  • 2nd offense → 3–7 day ban
  • 3rd offense → 14–30 day ban
  • extreme repeat abuse → permanent ban

4) CHAT & PLAYER CONDUCT

Includes spam, harassment, slurs, threats, NSFW, impersonation, false accusations, and harmful real-life statements.

Typical punishment ladder:

  • minor spam → 30m–6h mute
  • harassment / toxicity → 6h–24h mute, then 3–7 day bans
  • slurs / hate speech → 1–7 day mute or ban
  • threats / severe harmful behavior → 7–30 day ban
  • extreme repeat offenses → permanent ban

Safety-related threats may escalate instantly.

5) ECONOMY & FARM ABUSE

Includes alt farming, market manipulation, AFK profit abuse, plugin loopholes, bypassing taxes/cooldowns, and infinite money loops.

Typical punishment ladder:

  • 1st offense → 5–7 day ban + rollback
  • 2nd offense → 14–30 day ban + full balance reset
  • 3rd offense → permanent ban

All unfair wealth may be: deleted, rolled back, transferred back, removed from linked accounts, wiped from farms, storage, and machines. This includes x-ray wealth, alt-farmed money, exploit profit, economy bullying, and manipulated market gains.

6) STAFF & INVESTIGATIONS

Includes refusing freeze checks, hiding evidence, alt evasion, moving illegal items, or interfering with investigations.

Typical punishment ladder:

  • 1st offense → 30 day ban
  • 2nd offense → permanent ban

Helping another player evade punishment may be treated identically.

7) LOOPHOLE ABUSE

Using wording loopholes, plugin behavior, or technicalities to justify obvious abuse.

Typical punishment ladder:

  • 1st offense → 3–5 day ban
  • 2nd offense → 14 day ban
  • 3rd offense → 30 day ban
  • extreme repeat abuse → permanent ban

Final punishments are always based on context, evidence, scale, intent, prior history, and server damage caused. Staff reserve the right to escalate immediately in severe cases involving economy damage, mass griefing, ban evasion, or malicious exploit abuse.
